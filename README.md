A lightweight, offline-first task management application built with Flutter. Designed with a focus on simplicity and total user privacy, this app stores all data locally on your device and allows you to seamlessly export your task lists to JSON or CSV formats. No accounts, no cloud sync, just your tasks.

## ✨ Features

* **Zero Data Loss:** Tasks are saved instantly to the device's local storage. You can close the app or restart your phone without losing your list.
* **Frictionless Task Management:** Quickly add, complete, or delete tasks with a clean, intuitive user interface.
* **Data Portability:** Don't let your data get locked in. Export your entire task list with a single tap.
    * **JSON Export:** Ideal for developers or migrating raw data.
    * **CSV Export:** Perfect for viewing your tasks in Excel, Google Sheets, or any standard spreadsheet viewer.
* **Native Sharing:** Utilizes Android's native share sheet to let you save exports to Google Drive, email them, or store them directly in your phone's local files.
* **No Backend Required:** 100% client-side operation ensuring complete privacy.
